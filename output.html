Sales Strategies

    Leverage social media platforms (such as Instagram and Facebook) to showcase the products and share customer reviews and experiences.

    Offer promotions and discounts during holidays and special events, such as Valentine's Day, Christmas, and New Year's Eve.

    Host wine and champagne tastings and events in-store or at partner venues to educate customers about the products and create a memorable experience.

    Partner with local restaurants and hotels to feature the products on their menus and in-room offerings.

    Develop a loyalty program to reward repeat customers and incentivize them to refer new business.

Employment Needs

    Sales Representatives: Responsible for promoting and selling the products, developing relationships with customers, and meeting sales targets.

    Marketing Manager: Develops and implements marketing strategies to promote the products, analyzes market trends, and tracks the effectiveness of promotions and advertising campaigns.

    Event Coordinator: Plans and executes wine and champagne tastings and other events, coordinates with partners and vendors, and ensures a seamless customer experience.

    Sommelier: Provides expert knowledge and advice on wine and champagne selection, pairings, and serving techniques to customers and sales representatives.
